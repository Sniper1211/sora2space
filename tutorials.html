Featured Tutorial: Creating Marketing Videos

Step 1: Define Your Goal

Start by clearly defining what you want to achieve with your video. Are you promoting a product, explaining a service, or telling a story?

Step 2: Craft Your Prompt

Write a detailed text description including the setting, characters, actions, and emotions you want to convey.

Step 3: Choose Video Style

Select the appropriate video style that matches your brand and message. Consider corporate, creative, or social media styles.

Step 4: Generate and Refine

Generate your video and review the results. Make adjustments to your prompt if needed for optimal results.
